South Florida Weekend Guide

February 27 – March 1, 2026
Be where your feet are.
There's nowhere else like South Florida on a Friday night. The air is warm, the music is live, and the city is putting on a show whether you come or not. Might as well be there. Here's what's worth your time this week.
