Best Trivia Nights in Delray Beach

Delray Beach has a surprisingly good trivia scene. Whether you're a regular who shows up with a locked-in team every week, or you just want to grab a beer and test your knowledge on a random Tuesday, there are solid options all over town. Here are the spots worth checking out.

The Office Delray

201 E Atlantic Ave, Delray Beach, FL 33444

The Office is one of the most popular trivia spots on Atlantic Ave, and for good reason. It's a sleek gastropub with great food, strong drinks, and a trivia night that fills up fast. The crowd is a mix of after-work regulars and competitive teams who take it pretty seriously. Get there early if you want a good table.

theofficedelray.com

Park Tavern

32 SE 2nd Ave, Delray Beach, FL 33444

Park Tavern is a neighborhood bar with pool tables, craft cocktails, and weekly trivia that draws a loyal crowd. It's more laid-back than some of the Atlantic Ave spots, which makes it perfect if you want to play without the intensity. Good beer list too.

Tim Finnegans Irish Pub

2885 S Federal Hwy, Delray Beach, FL 33483

If you like your trivia with a Guinness and some Celtic music in the background, Tim Finnegans is your place. It's a proper Irish pub with dark wood, a great tap list, and trivia nights that feel like you're playing in Dublin (minus the weather). A little off the main drag on Federal Highway, which means it's easier to grab a spot.

timfinnegans.com

Saltwater Brewery

1701 W Atlantic Ave, Delray Beach, FL 33444

Saltwater is a laid-back craft brewery with a big outdoor space, food trucks, and a chill vibe. Their trivia nights are casual and fun, and you can bring your dog. The beer is solid (try the Screamin' Reels IPA), and the whole atmosphere is just easy. No pretension, no dress code, just good questions and cold beer.

saltwaterbrewery.com

Most of these spots run trivia weekly, but days and times can shift. Your best bet is to check The Active Owl's Delray Beach page for the current schedule so you're not showing up on the wrong night.
