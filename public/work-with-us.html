Why It Works

The difference between reach
and the right reach.

Instagram ads reach everyone. We reach people in your zip code who are actively asking "what should I do tonight?" That's not the same thing.

Intent over impressions

Our users open the app with a goal. They want to go somewhere. You don't have to convince them to go out — they're already going. You just have to show up.

Editorial credibility

We don't list every bar on Yelp. Featured businesses are hand-picked. Being on The Active Owl signals quality to our audience — not just a paid slot.

Newsletter placement

SEO visibility
