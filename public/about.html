About The Active Owl

The Active Owl is a South Florida activity guide built for people who want to know what's actually happening nearby, without sorting through a dozen apps or dead event pages. It covers day and night, all week, across five cities in Palm Beach County: Delray Beach, Boca Raton, Boynton Beach, Lake Worth Beach, and West Palm Beach.

How it works

Toggle between Day and Night mode to filter activities by time of day. Use the city switcher to jump between areas. Filter by category (yoga, live music, trivia, happy hour, etc.) or hit the dice for a random pick. Everything updates daily.

Who built it

The Active Owl was created by Tim Palladino, a South Florida local tired of scattered event listings and apps built for cities three times the size. It started as a simple tool to track brunch spots and trivia nights, and grew from there. If something's missing or wrong, send a note.

What's covered

Day activities include yoga, hiking, beach sports, farmers markets, water sports, and brunch. Night activities include bars, live music, DJ sets, happy hours, trivia, karaoke, comedy, and clubs. The goal is to cover the full range, not just the obvious stuff.

Expansion

Palm Beach County is the focus for now. Broward is next. If you want to see your city or venue added, reach out.
